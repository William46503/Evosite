Evolution Championship Serie

EVO(Evolution) is the Largest Fighting-Game event in Esports History

A rundown of EVO Championship Series

  • 1996 | Evo was found by Tom Cannon,started as "Battle by the Bay", a 40-man Super Street Fighter II Turbo and Street Fighter Alpha 2 tournament
  • 2001 | First "Battle by the Bay" to have a foreign champion
  • 2002 | As it got more popularity over the years, "Battle by the Bay" was renamed to todays "EVO"
  • 2004 | With over 700 competitors from all around the globe in over 9 titles, it was the birthplace of the "Evo Moment #37"
  • 2006 | The first year that Capcom gave its official, public support of the Evolution tournaments.
  • 2009 | Featuring the newly released Street Fighter 4. Evo attendance increased by a considerable margin,
    the tournament for it alone boasted more than 1000 participants
  • 2017 | As Evo has become a World Championship, Evo Japan was announced to be held in Japan for the first time in history
  • 2018 | First year of Evo Japan was held in Ikebukuro, Japan
  • 2019 | Evo took place on August 2 to 4 with the entire event being held at the Mandalay Bay resort with it's first Pakistani Champion in Tekken 7, Ashlan Ash.
